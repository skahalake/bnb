Outdoor Activities at Skaha Lakefront BnB

The stunning Okanagan region offers a plethora of outdoor activities for every type of adventurer. At Skaha Lakefront BnB, you'll find the perfect home base to enjoy a wide range of experiences, from thrilling watersports on Skaha Lake to exploring scenic hiking trails, mountain biking, skiing, and even world-class fishing.

Water Sports on Skaha Lake

Skaha Lake is a playground for water enthusiasts. Enjoy kayaking, paddleboarding, jet skiing, and fishing. With its calm, clear waters, it's perfect for both beginners and seasoned pros. Rentals are available for all equipment, making it easy to get on the water and make the most of your stay.

Fishing on Skaha Lake

Skaha Lake offers some of the best freshwater fishing in British Columbia. Whether you're casting from the shore or out on a boat, you'll find plenty of opportunities to catch bass, trout, and other species. The tranquil waters make it a relaxing way to spend your day surrounded by stunning scenery.

Hiking Trails

Explore the great outdoors with scenic hikes around Okanagan Falls and the surrounding area. From easy walks along the lake to challenging mountain trails, you'll find something for every level of hiker. The nearby Kettle Valley Railway Trail and Skaha Bluffs Provincial Park offer breathtaking views of the valley, vineyards, and lakes.

Mountain Biking

With access to world-class mountain biking trails, Okanagan Falls is a hub for cycling enthusiasts. Explore the South Okanagan Bike Trails and nearby Kettle Valley Railway bike trail. Whether you're a novice or an experienced rider, you'll find routes that offer stunning views and a great workout.

Skiing and Snowboarding

In the winter months, the region becomes a winter sports paradise. Big White Ski Resort, just a short drive from Skaha Lakefront BnB, is a popular destination for skiing and snowboarding. Offering miles of groomed runs, terrain parks, and incredible snow conditions, Big White is a must for winter sports enthusiasts. Other nearby resorts include Apex Mountain Resort.

Bird Watching

The Okanagan Valley is a prime location for bird watching, with numerous species migrating through the area year-round. Enjoy a peaceful walk through local parks and nature reserves like Skaha Bluffs Provincial Park or the Okanagan River Channel, where you can observe a variety of birds in their natural habitat.

Okanagan Wine Tours

Take a scenic tour through the region's renowned vineyards and wineries. The Okanagan Wine Tour offers guided tours that include tastings of world-class wines. Visit award-winning wineries like Kettle Valley Winery and Black Hills Estate Winery.
