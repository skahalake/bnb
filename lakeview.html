Lakeview Serenity

Experience the full comfort and luxury of Skaha Lakefront BnB by reserving the entire main floor vacation rental — perfect for families or couples traveling together. This spacious lakefront suite offers two beautiful bedrooms, multiple lounge areas, and incredible Skaha Lake views.

The Deluxe King Bedroom includes a private ensuite, while the Deluxe Queen Bedroom has a private bathroom located just next door. Please note: the queen room does not have an ensuite or private balcony. This setup offers privacy and space, making it an ideal configuration for two couples or a small family.

This is the best choice if you're looking to enjoy the entire main level of the home to yourself — including the cozy den, sunny breakfast area, elegant dining space, and expansive living room with lakefront windows.

For larger groups or complete privacy, you can book the Sunset Retreat on the walkout level along with this suite. That way, you’ll have the entire property to yourself — no shared spaces, just peaceful lakeside living for your whole family or group.
