Sunset Retreat

Take a peek inside your stay

Welcome to the Sunset Retreat, a spacious walkout-level suite at Skaha Lakefront BnB, offering serene comfort and stunning lake views. Located just steps from the lakeshore, this peaceful two-bedroom suite is ideal for families, couples, or friends looking to unwind by the water’s edge. Its walkout location makes it a great choice for guests who prefer to avoid stairs, offering easy access to both indoor and outdoor spaces.

The Sunset Retreat includes two beautifully appointed queen bedrooms, a shared Jack & Jill bathroom, a cozy family lounge, and a dining area with a mini-fridge. Step outside to enjoy your private patio or relax under the outdoor gazebo as you take in Skaha Lake’s spectacular sunset views. It's the perfect lakeside escape for creating lasting memories.
