Your Lakeside Escape Awaits 🌞

Welcome to Skaha LakeFront BnB — a charming, waterfront hideaway in the heart of BC wine country 🍷. Nestled along the shimmering shores of Skaha Lake in beautiful Okanagan Falls, this is where sunny days meet starry nights and your vacation finds its vibe.

Located at 476 Hody Drive, we’re the ideal launchpad for wine lovers, outdoor explorers, or anyone looking to swap city buzz for lake breezes. Whether you're here to sip 🍇, stroll 🚶, soak 🌊, or snooze 😴 — Skaha LakeFront BnB has your perfect pace.

Our Property 🏡

Set on 0.4 acres of prime lakefront, our fully renovated (2022) 3,943 sq. ft. home offers direct access to Skaha Lake and breathtaking west-facing sunsets 🌅. This is not a rustic cabin — it’s a thoughtfully curated space that feels more like visiting your favourite relative... who happens to have great taste and a house by the lake.

You’ll have access to four cozy and stylish bedrooms — a King room, Queen room, plus two Queen rooms on the walkout lower level. The home features four full bathrooms and a powder room, so no awkward hallway run-ins in the morning 🚿. (You’re welcome.)

  • Sunny sitting and family rooms with sweeping lake views 🌊
  • A no-pressure, no-cooking-needed service kitchen for light bites or your morning brew ☕
  • Cozy dining space — the kind where breakfast somehow tastes better 🍳
  • A covered deck perfect for golden hour sipping, stargazing, or soaking in the hot tub 🛁
  • A walkout lower level lounge when you need a moment of lakeside zen 🌿

Outdoor Spaces 🌳

Our outdoor setup? Equal parts relaxation station and “wow, I need a photo of this” 📸:

  • Two-tiered backyard with lush greenery, fruit trees, and gazebos with fire tables 🔥
  • A BBQ area — because someone always wants to be the grill master 🍔
  • Private lakefront access for a dip, a paddle, or just a sit-and-stare moment 🚣‍♀️
  • A stone peninsula with mooring — if you’re arriving James Bond-style by boat 🛥️
  • Ample guest parking and a public boat launch nearby 🚗

Location, Location, Relaxation 📍

Skaha LakeFront BnB is tucked right into the action (but not too much action):

  • Just 0.5 km from Okanagan Falls town centre for local eats, shops, and community charm 🛍️
  • Walking distance to Skaha Lake beach, the scenic KVR Trail, and lakefront parks 🏖️
  • Surrounded by world-class wineries: Naramata Bench, Black Sage Bench, Golden Mile, and the District Wine Village 🍇
  • Near Penticton, Oliver, Apex Ski Resort, and multiple golf courses — adventure in every direction ⛷️⛳

Make It a Moment 💍

Planning something special? Our lakeside backdrop is the perfect stage for unforgettable moments. From intimate weddings 💐 to bridal showers, milestone birthdays, and stylish gatherings — this space sets the scene. Think laughter, lake views, and love stories with a splash of wine 🍷.
