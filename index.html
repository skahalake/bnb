Welcome to Skaha LakeFront BnB — your cozy, lakefront getaway nestled in the heart of Okanagan wine country.

Located at 476 Hody Drive in Okanagan Falls, our charming bed & breakfast offers the perfect blend of tranquility, comfort, and convenience. Whether you're here to explore the region’s award-winning wineries, relax by the lake, or enjoy the outdoors, Skaha LakeFront BnB is the perfect place to stay.

Our Property

Set on 0.4 acres of pristine lakefront land, our home offers direct access to Skaha Lake and stunning west-facing views. Fully renovated in 2022, this spacious 3,943 sq. ft. home blends modern amenities with cozy charm.

We offer five beautifully appointed bedrooms — three on the main floor (King, Queen, and Double) and two Queen bedrooms on the walkout lower level. With four full bathrooms and a powder room, guests have plenty of space and privacy throughout their stay.

  • Bright sitting room and family room with lake views and smart TVs
  • Fully equipped kitchen ideal for gathering and entertaining
  • Cozy dining room with beautiful natural light
  • Spacious covered deck perfect for sunset views, outdoor dining, or a soak in the hot tub
  • Lower level includes additional bedrooms, lounge area, and smaller dining space

Outdoors

Our outdoor spaces are designed for relaxation and connection:

  • Two-tiered backyard with seating, fruit trees, and two gazebos with a fire table
  • BBQ area and direct lake access from our private shoreline
  • Large stone peninsula with mooring area
  • Public boat launch just down the street
  • Spacious front yard and ample guest parking

Ideal Location

Skaha LakeFront BnB is perfectly positioned for exploration:

  • Just 0.5 km from the Okanagan Falls town centre
  • Walking distance to Skaha Lake beach, KVR Trail, and local shops
  • Surrounded by wineries in Naramata Bench, Black Sage Bench, and Golden Mile, District Wine Village
  • Close to Penticton, Oliver, Apex Ski Resort, and several golf courses

Whether you're here to sip, stroll, swim, or simply unwind, Skaha LakeFront BnB offers a peaceful home base with all the comforts you need.
