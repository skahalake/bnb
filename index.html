Welcome to Skaha LakeFront BnB

Discover Skaha LakeFront BnB, a luxurious lakefront vacation rental in Okanagan Falls, British Columbia. Situated on the crystal-clear shores of Skaha Lake, this elegant retreat is the perfect getaway for couples, families, and small groups looking to explore the vibrant heart of the South Okanagan.

Our newly renovated home offers four spacious bedrooms, four bathrooms, a powder room, and expansive living areas designed for relaxation. Whether you're looking for a family-friendly Airbnb in the Okanagan or a peaceful retreat close to Penticton, our BnB provides the perfect balance of comfort and scenic beauty.

Explore a world of outdoor adventure right outside your door. Guests enjoy easy access to swimming, kayaking, paddleboarding, and fishing on Skaha Lake. Nearby trails offer excellent hiking and mountain biking, while the region is a paradise for bird watching and wildlife enthusiasts.

Just minutes away, indulge in the world-renowned wineries of the Okanagan Valley, featuring wine tastings, tours, and vineyard dining. Golfers will love nearby courses like St. Andrews by the Lake, while winter travelers are within reach of top ski resorts like Apex Mountain.

In August, join the fun at Penticton’s famous Peach Festival—a family-friendly celebration with parades, concerts, local food, and community spirit. Year-round, Okanagan Falls is a hub for local events, artisan markets, farm tours, and seasonal festivals.

Whether you’re planning a wine country weekend, a summer beach holiday in BC, or a cozy winter escape, Skaha LakeFront BnB is your home base for adventure, comfort, and unforgettable memories.
